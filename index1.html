Why Invest in the Alentejo Coast

The Alentejo Coast has become one of Southern Europe's most attractive destinations for luxury tourism and hospitality investment. Combining pristine beaches, protected landscapes, exceptional gastronomy and increasing international recognition, the region offers long-term value supported by limited supply and strong demand.

01

Prime Location

Positioned between Comporta, Melides and Sines, the estate benefits from one of Portugal's fastest growing tourism corridors while maintaining privacy and authenticity.

02

Luxury Tourism Growth

International luxury brands continue to invest in the region, attracting affluent visitors seeking nature, wellbeing and exclusive experiences.

03

Limited Supply

Strict planning regulations preserve the natural landscape, making approved hospitality developments increasingly rare and valuable.

04

Approved Development Rights

The approved PIP significantly reduces planning uncertainty and accelerates project execution, creating immediate investment value.

05

Sustainability

Cork oak forests, biodiversity and low-density development create an ideal setting for modern, environmentally responsible hospitality concepts.

06

Long-Term Appreciation

Continued international demand, infrastructure improvements and constrained land supply support strong long-term capital appreciation.
