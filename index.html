THE ESTATE

A Rare Investment Opportunity on Portugal's Alentejo Coast

Located just 30 minutes from the Atlantic Coast and approximately 90 minutes from Lisbon and the Algarve, São Domingos Reserve combines immediate agricultural income with a unique hospitality development opportunity in one of Portugal's most sought-after regions.

The 50-hectare estate benefits from an approved planning framework allowing the development of a Rural Hotel with up to 5,000 sqm of gross construction area, creating an exceptional opportunity for investors seeking exposure to Portugal's luxury tourism market.

Beyond its development potential, the property already generates recurring income through cork production. The young cork oak forest is expected to significantly increase its productivity over the coming years, while the estate also benefits from annual EU agricultural subsidies.

Combining immediate revenue, long-term capital appreciation and development potential, São Domingos Reserve represents a rare opportunity to acquire a strategic hospitality asset on the Alentejo Coast.

DEVELOPMENT OPPORTUNITY

A Hospitality Project Ready to Move Forward

São Domingos Reserve benefits from an approved planning framework allowing the development of a Rural Hotel with up to 5,000 sqm of gross construction area.

The subdivision process has been approved, providing a clear legal framework for the future development. The next planning stage consists of submitting the Prior Information Request (PIP), offering investors additional planning certainty before detailed architectural design.

The project offers exceptional flexibility, allowing the creation of a luxury boutique hotel, wellness retreat, nature resort or branded hospitality concept within one of Portugal's fastest-growing tourism destinations.

Large hospitality development opportunities of this scale have become increasingly scarce along the Alentejo Coast, making São Domingos Reserve a unique long-term investment.

LOCATION

Exceptional Connectivity Between Lisbon, Comporta and the Algarve

São Domingos Reserve is strategically positioned in the municipality of Santiago do Cacém, at the heart of Portugal's rapidly expanding Alentejo Coast.

The estate offers an exceptional balance between privacy and accessibility, located just minutes from São Domingos village and within comfortable driving distance of Lisbon, Comporta, Melides, Sines and the Algarve.

International investors benefit from easy access through Lisbon International Airport while enjoying one of Europe's last unspoilt coastal regions, increasingly recognised for luxury tourism and sustainable hospitality developments.
