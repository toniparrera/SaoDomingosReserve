THE ESTATE

A Rare Investment Opportunity on Portugal's Alentejo Coast

Located just 30 minutes from the Atlantic Coast and approximately 90 minutes from Lisbon and the Algarve, São Domingos Reserve combines immediate agricultural income with a unique hospitality development opportunity in one of Portugal's most sought-after regions.

The 50-hectare estate benefits from an approved planning framework allowing the development of a Rural Hotel with up to 5,000 sqm of gross construction area, creating an exceptional opportunity for investors seeking exposure to Portugal's luxury tourism market.

Beyond its development potential, the property already generates recurring income through cork production. The young cork oak forest is expected to significantly increase its productivity over the coming years, while the estate also benefits from annual EU agricultural subsidies.

Combining immediate revenue, long-term capital appreciation and development potential, São Domingos Reserve represents a rare opportunity to acquire a strategic hospitality asset on the Alentejo Coast.
